  • Diving suit

    We are sorry to say that underwater there won’t be fancy and highly fashionable clothes, but we will have reinforced dive suits that include the "GILLS" technology to breathe and a small oxygen tank in case of emergency.

    And you will always carry in yourself a modified tablet that is made for everyday use. This tablet will be your wallet, your ID and assistant. You can access the internet with it and many other things also.

    There will be many types of diving suits depending on the usage in a random situation. There will be 3 types of suits. The anti-radiation suit, reinforced diving suit, cooled suit and survival suit.

    An anti-radiation suit will be used if there is a nuclear failure with the reactor. You must be very calm and fast equipping this suit because it will reduce the radiation levels in your body to the optimal levels. And the most special one but least useable will be the survival suit.

    The reinforced diving suit is used for deep diving only. But we do not recommend to go near heat areas otherwise the suit will get damaged.

    The survival suit won't be made for deep diving. It will consume your sweat and urine and make it drinkable enough to get hydrated. It may sound gross but who knows, when you get lost and don’t have any source of water you have to eat and drink everything to survive.

    All of the suites have the “GILLS” technology in their rebreathers.
